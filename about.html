About Me

Izzy Rosina

I am a dedicated Photographer who exhibits a true love for the craft, art and science of the photography process who inspires others with creative insight and enthusiasm. I have always been a hard working and bright person, especially during all my volunteer and work experience. My photography journey started 6 years ago with a wonderful teacher inspiring me with her beautiful photography. From then on, I have been obsessed with all things photography, observing and taking in every detail of each photo I see.

I live in the UK and work mainly around the Midlands.
